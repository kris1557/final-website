Professional Goal

My professional goal is to move into a role centered on cloud computing, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence. Fields where I see the future of enterprise technology heading. I am drawn to organizations building and securing large-scale cloud infrastructure, and I want to be part of the shift toward AI-driven systems that monitor networks, detect threats, and automate the operations that used to require constant manual oversight.

What sets me apart is the foundation I bring from the AV/IT world. Working at the junction of AV control systems and network infrastructure has taught me how to troubleshoot complex, interconnected systems where hardware, software, and network behavior all have to work together flawlessly. AV and IT are not fields that obviously overlap on the surface, but I have spent my career proving that they do — and that same instinct for finding connections between seemingly unrelated technologies is exactly what I want to bring to cloud and security work, where infrastructure, automation, and intelligent systems increasingly depend on each other.

Over the next few years, I intend to earn my CompTIA Security+ and AWS certifications, alongside building a working foundation in AI and machine learning fundamentals, to complement my existing AVIXA CTS and Crestron credentials. My long-term target is a role in cloud security or AI-assisted infrastructure management.

Companies I Am Targeting

The following companies offer roles closely aligned with my background and career direction.

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — One of the leading cloud platform and the technology I am actively certifying in. A cloud support, security, or solutions architecture role here would put me directly inside the infrastructure I am studying at the company that defines the space.

  • CrowdStrike — A leader in AI-powered endpoint security and threat detection. CrowdStrike's platform is built around the same idea I want to specialize in: using artificial intelligence to detect and respond to threats faster than a human analyst could alone. A security engineering or threat detection role here aligns directly with my Security+ and AI goals.

  • Microsoft — A major force across cloud (Azure), security (Microsoft Defender, Sentinel), and AI (Copilot, Azure AI). Microsoft's scale across all three of my target areas makes it an ideal place to grow into a role that blends cloud infrastructure, security operations, and applied AI.

Each of these organizations values professionals who have a wide range of experience with traditional IT components while also looking towards future technology that will fundamentally affect the majority of this world. I want to be on the front lines of that brave new world.

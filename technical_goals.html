Technical Goals

Setting clear, time-bound learning goals is something I take seriously. As someone who works at the intersection of AV and IT, I know that the technical landscape moves fast. The three goals below reflect the skills I have identified as highest priority for advancing my career into cybersecurity and cloud infrastructure. Each goal has a defined start date and target completion date to keep me accountable.

CompTIA Security+ Certification

Security+ is the industry-recognized baseline certification for cybersecurity professionals and is often required for government and DoD-adjacent roles. I have been studying the domains through my coursework at APSU and a dedicated 10-week study plan. Passing this exam will validate my knowledge of threats, attacks, cryptography, network security, and identity management.

Start date: July 1, 2026 • Target completion: September 30, 2026

AWS Cloud Practitioner / Solutions Architect

Cloud infrastructure is now central to nearly every enterprise environment. I am studying EC2, S3, RDS, ELB, Auto Scaling, and VPC, and working toward earning the AWS Cloud Practitioner certification first, followed by the Solutions Architect Associate. This will position me to support hybrid cloud-physical AV/IT environments and move into cloud-oriented roles.

Start date: August 1, 2026 • Target completion: January 31, 2027

Artificial intelligence and machine learning fundamentals

AI is increasingly embedded in IT and cybersecurity tooling, from automated threat detection to AI-assisted network monitoring. My goal is to build a working understanding of machine learning fundamentals and how AI is applied in enterprise IT environments. I plan to complete an introductory AI/ML course and explore how AI tools are being integrated into the AV and security platforms I already use.

Start date: October 1, 2026 • Target completion: March 31, 2027
